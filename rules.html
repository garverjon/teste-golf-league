League Basics

  • Style: Two Man Scramble — fun first.

No Cheating!!!.

Scoring & Points

  • Standings include: Wins/Losses + Points.
  • Points (current): Match win = 2, Tie = 1 each.
  • Honesty rule: Keep it fair — we’re here for fun.

Pace of Play

  • Ready golf.
  • Keep up with the group ahead.
  • If a hole is blowing up, pick up and move on.
